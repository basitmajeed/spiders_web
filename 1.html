Destinations in Kashmir

Pahalgam

Pahalgam is a very nice place and a very famous tourist destination. It is around 100 KM far from the city of Srinagar. It has beautiful meadows and it has the famous Betaab Valley. It is also a staring point for the famous alpine lake treks of Tarsar and Marsar. Thousands of Hindu tourist visit every year in order to complete their pilgrimage.

Sonmarg

Who has not heard of Sonmarg? One of the best off beat and heat escape spot of Kashmir. I hosts the infamous Thajiwas glacier. Whenever you visit, you are bound to find snow, which is a treat to the eyes. It is also a destination stop to Ladakh.

Gulmarg

Gulmarg is the most famous tourist destination in my opinion. It is a great hillstation that hosts lots of horses, grass and the famous Gandola. One can travel from Phase-I to Phase-II where you are bound to find snow. It is a very cool place and a overall nice and relaxing place.
